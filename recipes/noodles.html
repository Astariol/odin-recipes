Vegan Butter Noodles

Description

Vegan Butter Noodles with a delicious butter sauce.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Vegan Butter
  • Garlic
  • Vegan Parmesan
  • Plant-based protein such as chickpeas

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot
  2. Cook pasta according to instructions
  3. Heat 2 tbsp vegan butter on a non-stick pan on medium-high heat and add minced garlic. cook 2-3 minutes until garlic is golden brown.
  4. Add al dente pasta to pan. Save approx. 1/2 cup of starch water from the pasta.
  5. Add the rest of the vegan butter, a splash of starch water and salt to taste. Add crushed chili flakes, parmesan or garlic powder to taste.
  6. Add your plant-based protein and enjoy!
