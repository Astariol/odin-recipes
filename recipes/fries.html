Air Fryer Waffle Fries

Description

A freshly made waffle fries recipe that can be air fried to perfection without being deep-fried or containing preservatives. Plus, for the price of store-bought waffle fries, you can get a big bag of potatoes and make 5x-10x more waffle fries at home! So it’s way cheaper & healthier to make them at home – win-win!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Seasoning of choice: I used Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes using a food slicer
  2. Place cut potatoes in a bowl of cold water
  3. Let potatoes soak about 5-10 minutes for maximum crispiness
  4. Drain potatoes
  5. Season uncooked fries and place in pre-heated air fryer
  6. Cook for 15 minutes tossing or flipping halfway through
  7. Enjoy with seasoning and condiments of choice!
